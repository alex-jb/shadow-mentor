procedure). Run only AFTER real device audio samples exist (media/voice-v8/device/).

artificiality · comprehension · trust calibration · pronunciation · preference. Also ask: missing info?
too-long sentence? too enthusiastic? authoritative beyond the evidence? would you interrupt? did the

small internal test; shorter duration is NOT a naturalness result.
