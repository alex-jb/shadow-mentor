Executive summary

Shadow is on track to be the first shipping AR compliance product for regulated banking. Three simultaneous claims hold:

  1. Voice interaction pattern is settled: gpt-realtime over WebRTC (200-350ms first-audio) as primary; Gemini 3.1 Flash Live as fallback for deictic references (“this loan”). No wake word — copy the visionOS 27 orb pattern shipping now.
  2. Hardware bet is settled: XREAL One Pro + Eye add-on ($99) + iPhone/Mac tether + WebXR is the safest 24-month path. Apple killed Vision Pro 2 (Kuo confirmed 2026-06-03); Vision Pro is publicly de-prioritized. XREAL Aura Fall 2026 is upgrade path but not July demo path.
  3. Three-vertical opportunity is real: Bank (July 2026), Trading (Q4 2026 via Orallexa backend), Data-Science (Q1 2027). No shipping product spans all three; academic term “context-aware AR” (Zhu 2504.16562, Chen 2412.12681) not adopted by any vendor. Shadow claims “Ambient Council” as the surface layer term.

Voice + AR interaction stack (July 2026 demo)

Primary voice model: gpt-realtime (OpenAI, released 2025-08). WebRTC transport, 200-350ms first-audio latency, native barge-in, MultiChallenge audio score 30.5% (up from 20.6% in prior generation). Reference: OpenAI Aug 2025, webrtcHacks latency benchmark.

Fallback voice model for deictic reference: Gemini 3.1 Flash Live. Best 1M-token multimodal context for “this loan” / “that flag” resolution across gaze + prior turns. Reference: Google Blog.

Disqualified: Claude Voice (no real interruption, turn-by-turn only, cutoff bugs GH #40345 + #47136). Reference: Anthropic Support docs.

Activation pattern: no wake word. Copy visionOS 27 Siri orb (June 2026 beta shipping now) — a gaze-anchored Council orb pinned above the physical loan PDF. Look-and-speak, 20-second always-listening window after each utterance. Neural-Band-style pinch as push-to-talk fallback for noisy demo rooms. References: Road to VR visionOS 27, MacRumors 2026-06-09.

Interaction contract: ambient conversation with native barge-in (“wait, back up”). Every AI answer anchored as visual overlay attached to the specific voice or flag being discussed per arXiv 2405.18537 (CHI 2024).

Compliance layer: persist voice queries + transcripts into Shadow’s hash-chain attestation (v1.5.10 primitive already exists). Treat every voice query as evidentiary. Disclose “you are speaking to an AI compliance council” per TX TRAIGA (Jan 2026) + FCC 2024 declaratory ruling at session start.

Three-vertical roadmap

Bank Mode (July 2026 demo, Q4 2026 GTM)

  • Product: Shadow v3 Ambient Council on XREAL One Pro + Eye + iPhone.
  • Audience: Chief Compliance Officer + Chief Audit Executive at mid-tier banks (Raymond James, Stifel, LPL, Houlihan Lokey, Lazard MM). Not JPM/GS (they build in-house).
  • Pricing: $2K/seat/year. Comparable: Vanta $15-60K/year, Drata $15-75K/year, enterprise SOC 2 $600K+.
  • 2027 realistic TAM: 30 mid-tier banks × 20 seats × $2K = $1.2M ACV path to $5M by 2028 if Big-4 channel partnership lands (Deloitte Omnia serves 85K auditors; PwC Aura; KPMG Clara). References: Deloitte Omnia, KPMG Clara, Pento 2025 review, ItBudgetCalculator.
  • Regulatory anchor: SR 26-2 Tier 3 explicitly carves GenAI/agentic AI out — Shadow fills the gap.

Trading Mode (Q4 2026 — Orallexa backend)

  • Product: Shadow surface + Orallexa backend (github.com/alex-jb/orallexa-ai-trading-agent, LangGraph 5-voice Bull/Bear/Judge + Critic + Polyseer, 745+ tests).
  • Audience: Chief Risk Officer, not the trader. Compliance sidecar on the desk, not Bloomberg-seat replacement.
  • Anchor: Bloomberg / broker screen. Risk-Officer voice surfaces VaR/ES violation pill anchored to position line; AML/KYC voice surfaces tipping-off flags anchored to counterparty row.
  • Direct competitor: Pillar ($20M a16z seed Apr 2026) — but they don’t have AR. Shadow’s differentiator is the surface layer + attestation chain.

Data-Science Mode (Q1 2027)

  • Product: Shadow surface + calibration engine on Jupyter cell / Cursor buffer anchor. Fair Lending voice flags protected-class proxy features inline (per v1.5.9 32 adversarial tests).
  • Audience: ML validator, model validation officer. Quiet but defensible — SR 26-2 Tier 3 explicitly carves out GenAI, no incumbent owns “council-based validation.”
  • Reference customer: Loredana’s Columbia statistics faculty introduction (2026-07-06 promise); Prof. Yang’s capstone cohort.

Post-deep-research on CFPB Circular 2022-03/2023-03 + FFIEC ECOA procedures + SR 26-2 replacement letter + FinCEN CDD Rule + tipping-off provisions, all 5 core persona rationale strings updated with real regulatory examiner voice.

All 5 personas updated. 706/707 tests pass, zero regressions.

Design references (Ready Player One + Sony + Android XR + Apple)

Adopt from RP1: Kinetic HUD summon on button/context trigger + dissolve on decision. Warm/cold faction color language (approve = warm, block = cold). References: Territory Studio, fxguide.

Adopt from Sony: 360 Reality Audio object-based spatial-audio primitive. 5 personas as 5 spatial audio object sources in a 145° arc around reviewer. References: Sony 360 RA overview.

Adopt from Android XR: WebXR delivery. Same build runs Galaxy XR + Aura + Vision Pro Safari + Chrome desktop. References: Android Developers WebXR, XREAL Aura announcement.

Adopt from Apple Vision Pro: Environment-anchor everything, never head-lock. Gaze-hover + pinch-select for XREAL Eye users. Windows anchored to space, not head. References: Apple HIG for visionOS, Ken Pfeuffer gaze-pinch.

Reject: Vision Pro Persona avatars (uncanny + breaks Severance restraint), Ready Player One haptic suit (fiction), Sony XYN ring controller (separate hardware), sticky head-locked overlay (Apple HIG anti-pattern), Gemini auto-spatialize on demo path (Android-only cuts Apple auditors out).
