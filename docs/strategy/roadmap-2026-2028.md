## Executive summary

Shadow is on track to be the first shipping AR compliance product for regulated banking. Three simultaneous claims hold:

1. **Voice interaction pattern is settled**: gpt-realtime over WebRTC (200-350ms first-audio) as primary; Gemini 3.1 Flash Live as fallback for deictic references ("this loan"). No wake word — copy the visionOS 27 orb pattern shipping now.
2. **Hardware bet is settled**: XREAL One Pro + Eye add-on ($99) + iPhone/Mac tether + WebXR is the safest 24-month path. Apple killed Vision Pro 2 (Kuo confirmed 2026-06-03); Vision Pro is publicly de-prioritized. XREAL Aura Fall 2026 is upgrade path but not July demo path.
3. **Three-vertical opportunity is real**: Bank (July 2026), Trading (Q4 2026 via Orallexa backend), Data-Science (Q1 2027). No shipping product spans all three; academic term "context-aware AR" (Zhu 2504.16562, Chen 2412.12681) not adopted by any vendor. Shadow claims "Ambient Council" as the surface layer term.

## Voice + AR interaction stack (July 2026 demo)

**Primary voice model:** `gpt-realtime` (OpenAI, released 2025-08). WebRTC transport, 200-350ms first-audio latency, native barge-in, MultiChallenge audio score 30.5% (up from 20.6% in prior generation). Reference: [OpenAI Aug 2025](https://openai.com/index/introducing-gpt-realtime/), [webrtcHacks latency benchmark](https://webrtchacks.com/measuring-the-response-latency-of-openais-webrtc-based-real-time-api/).

**Fallback voice model for deictic reference:** Gemini 3.1 Flash Live. Best 1M-token multimodal context for "this loan" / "that flag" resolution across gaze + prior turns. Reference: [Google Blog](https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-1-flash-live/).

**Disqualified:** Claude Voice (no real interruption, turn-by-turn only, cutoff bugs GH #40345 + #47136). Reference: [Anthropic Support docs](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/11101966-use-voice-mode).

**Activation pattern:** no wake word. Copy visionOS 27 Siri orb (June 2026 beta shipping now) — a gaze-anchored Council orb pinned above the physical loan PDF. Look-and-speak, 20-second always-listening window after each utterance. Neural-Band-style pinch as push-to-talk fallback for noisy demo rooms. References: [Road to VR visionOS 27](https://roadtovr.com/siri-on-vision-pro-is-getting-eye-tracked-activation-and-visual-awareness-alongside-new-ai-features/), [MacRumors 2026-06-09](https://www.macrumors.com/2026/06/09/visionos-27-siri-ai-eye-aware-notifications/).

**Interaction contract:** ambient conversation with native barge-in ("wait, back up"). Every AI answer anchored as visual overlay attached to the specific voice or flag being discussed per arXiv 2405.18537 (CHI 2024).

**Compliance layer:** persist voice queries + transcripts into Shadow's hash-chain attestation (v1.5.10 primitive already exists). Treat every voice query as evidentiary. Disclose "you are speaking to an AI compliance council" per TX TRAIGA (Jan 2026) + FCC 2024 declaratory ruling at session start.

## Three-vertical roadmap

### Bank Mode (July 2026 demo, Q4 2026 GTM)

- **Product:** Shadow v3 Ambient Council on XREAL One Pro + Eye + iPhone.
- **Audience:** Chief Compliance Officer + Chief Audit Executive at mid-tier banks (Raymond James, Stifel, LPL, Houlihan Lokey, Lazard MM). Not JPM/GS (they build in-house).
- **Pricing:** $2K/seat/year. Comparable: Vanta $15-60K/year, Drata $15-75K/year, enterprise SOC 2 $600K+.
- **2027 realistic TAM:** 30 mid-tier banks × 20 seats × $2K = **$1.2M ACV** path to $5M by 2028 if Big-4 channel partnership lands (Deloitte Omnia serves 85K auditors; PwC Aura; KPMG Clara). References: [Deloitte Omnia](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/deloitte-unveils-connected-agentic-intelligence-in-deloitte-omnia-to-advance-audit--assurance-302808361.html), [KPMG Clara](https://kpmg.com/xx/en/media/press-releases/2025/04/kpmg-advances-ai-integration-in-kpmg-clara-smart-audit-platform.html), [Pento 2025 review](https://www.pento.ai/blog/a-year-of-mcp-2025-review), [ItBudgetCalculator](https://itbudgetcalculator.com/financial-services-it-budget).
- **Regulatory anchor:** SR 26-2 Tier 3 explicitly carves GenAI/agentic AI out — Shadow fills the gap.

### Trading Mode (Q4 2026 — Orallexa backend)

- **Product:** Shadow surface + Orallexa backend (`github.com/alex-jb/orallexa-ai-trading-agent`, LangGraph 5-voice Bull/Bear/Judge + Critic + Polyseer, 745+ tests).
- **Audience:** Chief Risk Officer, not the trader. Compliance sidecar on the desk, not Bloomberg-seat replacement.
- **Anchor:** Bloomberg / broker screen. Risk-Officer voice surfaces VaR/ES violation pill anchored to position line; AML/KYC voice surfaces tipping-off flags anchored to counterparty row.
- **Direct competitor:** [Pillar ($20M a16z seed Apr 2026)](https://techcrunch.com/2026/04/14/financial-risk-management-platform-pillar-raises-20m-seed-in-round-led-by-a16z/) — but they don't have AR. Shadow's differentiator is the surface layer + attestation chain.

### Data-Science Mode (Q1 2027)

- **Product:** Shadow surface + calibration engine on Jupyter cell / Cursor buffer anchor. Fair Lending voice flags protected-class proxy features inline (per v1.5.9 32 adversarial tests).
- **Audience:** ML validator, model validation officer. Quiet but defensible — SR 26-2 Tier 3 explicitly carves out GenAI, no incumbent owns "council-based validation."
- **Reference customer:** Loredana's Columbia statistics faculty introduction (2026-07-06 promise); Prof. Yang's capstone cohort.

## Design references (Ready Player One + Sony + Android XR + Apple)

**Adopt from RP1:** Kinetic HUD summon on button/context trigger + dissolve on decision. Warm/cold faction color language (approve = warm, block = cold). References: [Territory Studio](https://territorystudio.com/project/ready-player-one/), [fxguide](https://www.fxguide.com/fxfeatured/ready-player-one-inside-the-oasis/).

**Adopt from Sony:** 360 Reality Audio object-based spatial-audio primitive. 5 personas as 5 spatial audio object sources in a 145° arc around reviewer. References: [Sony 360 RA overview](https://www.whathifi.com/advice/sony-360-reality-audio-everything-you-need-to-know).

**Adopt from Android XR:** WebXR delivery. Same build runs Galaxy XR + Aura + Vision Pro Safari + Chrome desktop. References: [Android Developers WebXR](https://developer.android.com/develop/xr/web), [XREAL Aura announcement](https://www.xreal.com/us/blog/project-aura-google-io-2026).

**Adopt from Apple Vision Pro:** Environment-anchor everything, never head-lock. Gaze-hover + pinch-select for XREAL Eye users. Windows anchored to space, not head. References: [Apple HIG for visionOS](https://developer.apple.com/design/human-interface-guidelines/designing-for-visionos/), [Ken Pfeuffer gaze-pinch](https://medium.com/antaeus-ar/design-principles-issues-for-gaze-and-pinch-interaction-a95e251169ae).

**Reject:** Vision Pro Persona avatars (uncanny + breaks Severance restraint), Ready Player One haptic suit (fiction), Sony XYN ring controller (separate hardware), sticky head-locked overlay (Apple HIG anti-pattern), Gemini auto-spatialize on demo path (Android-only cuts Apple auditors out).
