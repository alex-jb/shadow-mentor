## Pre-flight (≤ 10 minutes once before recording)

- [ ] Clean desktop background (no folder clutter visible behind Chrome)

- [ ] Shadow demo `index.html` open and tested — all 4 devices switchable, all 5 personas switchable, all 4 scenarios switchable, WiFi toggle works
- [ ] Audio test: 5-second clip, listen back, confirm no echo / fan noise / room compression
- [ ] System DND ON, Slack DND ON, Messages DND ON
- [ ] Loom set to capture **window only** (Chrome), not full screen
- [ ] One pre-staged decision typed into a text editor: "We're considering an LBO of AcmeCo. Term Loan B at SOFR + 425 is being offered to us in syndication."
- [ ] One reference URL in clipboard: `github.com/alex-jb/embodied-compliance-council`

## Recording rules

- **Per-segment two-take maximum.** Past that, you're overthinking it. Use the best of two.
- **No edits in post except trim.** Only trim leading + trailing dead air.
- **5 full seconds of pause** on the JARVIS scene at 3:45. That silence is the demo.
- **Watch back at 1.5× before posting** — if 1.5× feels rushed, you talked too fast and need to re-record.

## Script (5 minutes, beat-by-beat)

### 0:00 – 0:30 — Hook (no face on camera, voice over the screen)

"Compliance officers at mid-tier banks review thirty to fifty loan files a day. By three pm, every file looks template-identical, and OCC examiners want contemporaneous evidence that the officer actually considered the alternative interpretation. Existing AI tools — Glean, Microsoft Copilot, Cluely — are cloud-only, persona-agnostic, and brand-poisoned for compliance use. Shadow is the on-device AI council and audit chain that follows the user across desktop, smart glasses, and spatial AR — local-only, persona-specific, audit-grade. Five minutes."

Click into Shadow demo. The default state (Desktop + Compliance + LBO scenario) is visible.

### 0:30 – 1:30 — Compliance, Desktop, LBO scenario

"This is the desktop client running locally on an Apple Silicon laptop. The analyst — a first-year at a mid-tier wealth-management firm — is looking at an LBO model in Excel. Shadow recognizes the screen, surfaces the Senior Leverage Ratio cell, and gives three voices."

Hover the panel briefly to draw attention.

"Junior analyst translates the jargon. Senior VP says what the boss actually cares about — the three-year trajectory, not the snapshot. Compliance flags the firm's underwriting Policy 4.3 ceiling at 4.5x and notes we're 30bps under. Every voice debate is hash-chained for audit."

Click the "Ask Shadow" button.

"That Q&A pair just went into a SHA-256 hash chain. OCC examiner asks 'did you really review this?' — analyst hands them the chain export."

### 1:30 – 2:30 — Device switching: same engine, different cognitive density

"Now watch the device picker."

Click "👓 Even G2" — the panel becomes the green monocular text-only mode.

"Same backend brain. Even Realities G2 — five-hundred-and-ninety-nine-dollar smart glasses with no camera. The analyst can wear these all day in a customer meeting. The compliance voice whispers via bone conduction. The customer never sees a screen."

Click "🕶 Brilliant Frame".

"Brilliant Labs Frame — three-forty-nine, open-SDK, color HUD. Two voices, with the camera scanning real-world documents. Internal use only — not customer-facing."

Click "✨ XReal Air 2 Ultra".

"XReal Air 2 Ultra. Six-ninety-nine. Six-degree-of-freedom spatial AR in a sunglass form factor. This is JARVIS mode."

Pause 5 full seconds. Let the floating panels register. Don't fill the silence.

"Three floating panels anchored to the analyst's real workspace. Risk Surface, Bias Constellation showing the last thirty decisions clustered, Counterparty Network with fifty-two-thousand nodes. Only the analyst sees these through the AR glasses. Bone-conduction council whispers, hash-chained audit. This is the form factor for quants and traders at their own desk."

### 2:30 – 3:30 — Persona switching: same screen, five professional perspectives

Click back to "🖥 Desktop" + "📊 LBO Model". Click each persona button in sequence: Compliance → Quant → Engineer → Trader → Advisor. About 12 seconds per persona. Read the new junior voice text aloud each time.

"Same screen, five entirely different domains of expertise. Shadow is one product. Five persona packs at separate seat prices. A mid-tier bank with a thousand employees deploys five persona packs from one enterprise license. About nine hundred eighty thousand dollars annually. Inside the ATD per-employee training budget at twelve to thirteen hundred per analyst."

### 3:30 – 4:00 — Local mode + WiFi disconnect proof

Click "Local" mode button. Then click the WiFi-off button.

"Local mode. Everything running on the laptop's NPU. No cloud, no PII upload. EU AI Act high-risk obligations entry-into-force August second, twenty twenty-six. Cluely cannot do this. Microsoft Copilot cannot do this. Glean cannot do this."

WiFi is off, local mode is on, the demo still responds. Show a fresh scenario click.

"Disconnected from the internet. Demo still working. That is the moat."

### 4:00 – 4:30 — Cryptographic audit chain

Click "Mark logged" to bump the audit counter.

"Every challenge, every response, every override, every gesture — hash-chained on-device. Exportable to OCC, CFPB, FINRA, EU AI Act Article 14 formats. The audit artifact regulators are about to demand on every decision in your first ninety days at the bank."

### 4:30 – 5:00 — Pricing, target, ask

Stop demo. Pause briefly.

"Pricing. Base engine, hundred and twenty thousand annually, no seat cap. Persona packs, fifteen hundred to twenty-four hundred per analyst per year. Hardware — G2 at five-ninety-nine, XReal at six-ninety-nine — separate one-time per user."

"Beachhead. Five mid-tier wealth-management firms by Q4 twenty twenty-six. Not Goldman, not JPMorgan, not Citi — they will build it in-house. We're going to Raymond James, Stifel, LPL, Houlihan Lokey, Lazard Middle Market, William Blair."

"If you run an analyst development program at a mid-tier US bank or wealth firm and the August second EU AI Act deadline is on your calendar, this video is the qualifying conversation. Reply to this email. I'll send the fifteen-page canonical product proposal and we can put a fifteen-minute call on the books for next week."
