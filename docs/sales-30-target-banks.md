## July cold-email template (canonical v1.0)

**Subject A (Compliance angle, default)**:

**Subject B (EU AI Act angle, if firm has EU exposure)**:
> EU AI Act Article 14 entry-into-force August 2 — your analyst class will need an audit chain

**Subject C (Direct, time-sensitive)**:
> 4-week pilot, [Firm Name] 2027 summer analyst class — $1,800 / analyst

---

**Body** (~180 words, single paragraph density to fit mobile preview):

Hi [First Name],

I'm reaching out because [Firm Name]'s investment in early-career analyst development is well documented (Stifel + Raymond James + Houlihan have all expanded analyst classes through 2026), and the August 2 EU AI Act entry-into-force is going to make "show me you actually reviewed this loan" a contemporaneous OCC evidence requirement rather than a memo-style retrospective.

**Reviewer notes**:

1. Personalize `[Firm Name]` and `[First Name]` per send. Use Apollo.io or LinkedIn Sales Navigator to enrich the buyer's name. If unavailable, use "Hi there —" and reference "your firm" instead of `[Firm Name]`.
2. The Loom demo should be a 5-minute walk-through of the Shadow demo + Bloomberg HUD + WiFi-off proof. Record before sending.
3. The canonical product proposal PDF lives at `~/Desktop/shadow-mentor/docs/shadow-product-proposal.pdf`. Attach to reply, do not attach to first cold email.
4. **Send cadence**: 5 emails per day for 6 days = full 30-list complete in 6 sends. Track in Notion or a CSV.
5. **Follow-up**: +5 business days, single short reply with a specific question ("Is the analyst development budget set at the firm level or at the practice level?") — open-question follow-ups outperform reminders.
6. **GDPR / CCPA**: include a `mailto:` unsubscribe line in footer. Required for cold US B2B as of CAN-SPAM + state laws.
