# Shadow spatial user study — scoring

Per task: accuracy (0/1 vs the correct_answer_source), completion_time (s), wrong_selection count,
backtracking count. Per condition: mean accuracy, median time, error rate.

Subjective (post-condition): NASA-TLX perceived workload; trust-calibration (does the participant
correctly say the chain proves integrity NOT correctness?); comfort (1-5); preference ranking.

Spatial-only (C/D): head-movement proxy (rotations to complete), recenter count.

Primary hypothesis-neutral analysis: report per-condition means + CIs; NO claim of superiority is made
without data. The key comprehension metric is T4 (integrity != correctness) accuracy across conditions.
